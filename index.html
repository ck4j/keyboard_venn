Venn

The Venn is a 65% keyboard that combines the aesthetics from my first keyboard design, the Chiwi60 and the 7V.

The aim of this design is to create something that is easily recognisable from all angles without relying on engravings or other embellishments. Image courtesy of Q"neatntidy".

SELECT COLOR

DETAILS

  • Case Angle: 7.0°
  • Materials: Aluminium & Stainless Steel
  • Colours: Silver, Olive, Burgundy, Navy
  • Mounting method: Leaf-spring sandwich
  • Plate: 1.5mm POM
  • PCB: WT65-G & USB-C daughterboard by Wilba-configurable with QMK/VIA
